Little Rainbow Mountain

Little Rainbow Mountain from Storm Lake is just shy of 7 miles, with an elevation gain of nearly 1,900ft. Definitely a fan favorite!

Last ran Sept 7, 2021

Cuttaway Pass

Level of difficulty increases on this one! Cuttaway pass is an 18-mile loop with jaw-breaking views of Warren Peak, West Goat Peak and the Mahoney Basin. With an elevation gain over 3,000ft, there will be no shortage of photo-snapping!

Last ran June 8, 2021

Pintler Ridge Run

Pintler Ridge run is truly the ultimate, and not for the faint of heart. This 50k has an elevation gain over 13,000ft, tackling several peaks of the Pintlers, such as Mount Howe, Mount Evans, Little Rainbow Mountain, and many more. You'll feel as if you are on the top of the world all day!

Last ran July 23, 2020
